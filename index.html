About
📊
Budget
Monthly income overview. Set your expected income, essential, discretionary, and saving targets. Track recommended vs actual spending percentages and manage upcoming savings funds.
📋
Expense Planning
Your biweekly paycheck tracker. Set your bank balance, check off bills as you pay them, and see your Current, Short, and Long term standings update in real time. Add adjustments for CC payments, upcoming income, or one-off expenses.
🏠
Costs
Your master bill register. Every bill lives here — name, company, due date, amount, pay method, autopay, and essential status. Edits here automatically update Expense Planning. Includes a summary breakdown by pay method, priority, and week.
🎯
Goals
Track your financial goals across three tiers — Must-Do, Good to Have, and Nice to Have. Tap any goal to cycle its status (green/yellow/red). Long press to reorder. Add new goals and assign them a tier and category.
📅
Paycheck Schedule
Your full biweekly pay calendar. Set a default paycheck amount and apply it to all future periods. Tap any row to override a specific period. Lock individual paychecks to protect them from the Apply All button.
🛡️
Essential & Emergency Fund
Track your emergency fund balance against 1-6 month milestones. See a full category breakdown of essential vs non-essential spending. Tap the balance to update it anytime.
